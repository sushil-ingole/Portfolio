  • Jealousy

    23, 2020

    The thing about jealousy is that when you find someone doing something soo good but then till that point it is always okay, the part of jealousy starts when we start to think about us doing the same thing like others within our mind, and mostly we get jealous to someone even before we trying to attempt the same thing with our whole dedication, and still we already try to think that damn I'll not be able to do this, like this thinking process we don't do consciously but our subconscious mind does that. So instead of feeling happy for them, jealousy comes in between, because we immediately try to compare ourself to the other person without giving our full attempt to do that thing and coming to a conclusion that yeah if I would try it then I could have also done that. So the thing is try to accept the things as they are, like someone is doing something out of their creative ideas, which is making them happy and which you can also do if you wanted to or it's just a god gift to them the same way you're gifted with something, but it's all about individual's wants what they choose to do and in which thing they want to explore themselves with full dedication and intensities. So don't ever feel jealous before coming to a conclusion without giving a try to that thing and respect the people that they choose something which is making us feel good cause that's why only we got jealous somewhere know and they're improving in their life in a positive way which you can also do if you want. So it's always about our choice.
